Fun Greens Africa was established in 2018 with the goal of introducing turf grass surfaces in the country. The founder of the company, Ajay Shah, a professional golfer has spent many years abroad playing at a professional level in his chosen sport. When he entered the international scene, he felt at a slight disadvantage, playing on surfaces that he was not used to in Africa. On his return to Kenya, he noticed this same disadvantage was there in many other sports. Playing surfaces were uneven and athletes faced the challenge of ball control. This inspired him to introduce the kind of turf grass surfaces into the local market that one would typically find in the international scene.
